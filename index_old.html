Who Am I?

Mathematician, programmer, casual musician.

I'm never quite sure what to write in these things.

Mathematics:

From 2011 to 2014 I was a PhD student in Mathematics at Southampton University, working in the intersection between algebra and topology. To be more specific, looking at modules over a category and their finiteness conditions, classifying spaces for families of subgroups, and related things. I received my PhD in October 2014.

Some preprints of mine are available on the arXiv.

For more mathsy things, here is an archive of my academic website at Southampton University.
